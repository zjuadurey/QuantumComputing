Shadow Hamiltonian Simulation POC - Direct Fourier Evolution
============================================================

Method:
  Shadow simulation uses direct Fourier-space evolution:
    psi_k(t) = psi_k(0) * exp(-i k² t / 2)
  This is the SAME Hamiltonian H = k²/2 as the baseline quantum circuit.
  The shadow is "exact" for this free-particle case.

Configuration:
  Grid size: 16x16
  Qubits: nx=4, ny=4
  Fourier block: K=2, size=5x5
  Feature dimension: 50
  Time points: 10, dt=0.1

Runtime:
  Baseline (quantum circuit): 1.878 seconds
  Shadow (direct FFT): 0.003214 seconds
  Speedup: 584x

Errors (shadow vs baseline):
  Mean feature error: 8.272580e-16
  Mean density error: 9.859009e-16
  Mean energy error: 1.394701e-15
  Max feature error: 1.658081e-15
  Max density error: 1.721978e-15
  Max energy error: 3.090109e-15

Note:
  Small errors are due to numerical precision differences between
  quantum circuit simulation and direct FFT computation.
